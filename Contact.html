About Me


While attending Sammamish Highschool, I began my photography career. I was offered a sales position at Kit's Camera. It wasn't long before I found myself working in the 1-hour photo booth due to my passion for developing and printing photos. There is a good chance I have printed some of your photos during your film days in Factoria, Issaquah, South Center, and Klahanie.

Over the years, shooting a variety of events, weddings, commercial products, I came to the realization that my passion is photographing architecture and interiors.

Feel free to contact me via email, phone or text. You can also use the form on the website.
